About Our Company

KARIHOME SARL is a leading construction company established in Morocco, specializing in building, construction, and renovation. Our vision is to be among the top construction companies in the Kingdom of Morocco.

Our Vision

KARIHOME's vision involves building long-term relationships with clients and partners who consider us integral to their success, leveraging our expertise, skills, and resources.

Our Values

Our Commitment

Our Services

Construction & Building

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our company and services.

What is the company's legal form and name?

KARIHOME is legally registered as a limited liability company (SARL) in Kenitra, Morocco under commercial registration number 72857, with the legal name "KARIHOME".

How are your services priced?

Our pricing is based on:
  • Submitted construction plans required for the project
  • Project type and specifications

What services do you offer?

We execute construction projects and interior design, while providing furnishing services and supplying construction materials.

What documents are required when signing a contract?

Required documents include:
  • Civil engineer's plans
  • Survey plans
  • Property deed or utilization authorization
  • Building permit (or approval minutes for implementation plans)
  • Commercial register and articles of association for legal entities
  • National ID or passport for individuals
  • Construction site logbook
  • Worksite insurance certificate
